# German Comparison Copy Design

Rewrite the German copy for the comparison hub and both competitor detail pages so it sounds native, concrete, and trustworthy instead of translated or AI-generated.

## Scope

- `/vergleiche`
- `/vergleiche/yazio-alternative`
- `/vergleiche/fddb-alternative`

## Problems To Fix

- too many abstract phrases like `Plattformlogik`, `Motivationsebenen`, or `ruhiger Tracker`
- some sentences feel translated from English product marketing
- parts of the copy over-explain instead of stating the actual comparison clearly
- several lines do not sound like natural German product copy

## Approved Direction

- direct
- native German
- concrete
- slightly blunt where the USP matters
- not salesy
- not overly polished

## Copy Principles

- Prefer practical user questions over abstract framing:
  - `Abo oder nicht?`
  - `Konto nötig oder nicht?`
  - `Was ist direkt in der App drin?`
- Keep sentences shorter.
- Avoid synthetic-sounding abstractions.
- Make the difference between Intake and Yazio / FDDB understandable without sounding performative.

## Structure Constraints

- keep the same page structure
- keep the same slugs and routing
- keep the comparison table layout
- rewrite wording only
