## Your calorie goal now has a clear color status

The calorie ring now changes color depending on how many calories you have left, or how far you are above your goal.
It stays green while you are within your goal, turns yellow when you are up to 10% above it, and turns red after that.
The same status color also appears in the weekly overview, giving you a quick snapshot of your week.

This feature is completely optional. You can turn it off at any time in Settings under Appearance.

![Color-coded calorie ring](assets/colors-ring-ios.mp4)

## Improvements to weight statistics

The weight statistics view has been revised and is now easier to read.

## Weekly overview for Android

It took a while, but the Android app now also gets the popular weekly overview at the top of the screen.
That makes it faster to move through your week and gives you a compact overview of your calorie goal status.

![Weekly overview on Android](assets/colors-android.mp4)

## Remove products from Frequent and Recent

You can now remove products from Frequent and Recent by long-pressing the product and choosing "Remove".
If you use that product again later, it can appear in the list again.

![Remove product](assets/delete.mp4)

## Swiping between days is now optional

Both apps now let you disable swiping between days. You can find the setting under Appearance.

![Appearance settings](assets/settings.png)

## Nutrition overview

Identical products are now grouped together in the nutrition overview.

## Bug fixes and improvements

As always, this release also includes a number of fixes for issues you keep reporting. Keep them coming :)

Among other things, the limit for Frequent and Recent has been increased to 100 products, giving you more room for your favorites.

You can find the full changelog [here](https://featurevoting.tobibechtold.dev/app/intake/changelog).
