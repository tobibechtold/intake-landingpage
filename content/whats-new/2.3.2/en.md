## Your day can now be shared

Intake can now create social-media-ready images for your stories or posts, so you no longer have to take a screenshot.
Your values for the current day are neatly prepared and ready to share.

![sharing](assets/sharing.mp4)

## Meals and macros stay expanded

Meals and macros now remember whether they were expanded or collapsed, even after restarting the app.

## Copy and duplicate recipes

You can now duplicate recipes. Just tap a recipe and choose Duplicate at the top. Intake creates an exact copy that you
can edit right away. The recipe is only created once you tap Save.

You can also duplicate a recipe from the overview by long-pressing it.

![duplicate recipes](assets/duplicate.mp4)

## Convert steps into calories

This setting is meant for users who track steps with their phone instead of a smartwatch. In some cases, only the steps
are saved, but they are not converted into calories. You should only enable this setting if you notice that your steps
are not counted toward your calorie goal.

If you use a smartwatch, this is usually already handled for you. Please check before enabling the setting to avoid
counting calories twice.

![steps](assets/steps.png)

## Bug fixes and improvements

As always, this release also includes a number of fixes for issues you keep reporting. Keep them coming :)

Among other things, this update fixes an issue that could cause the app to crash. It also fixes a bug in History and
Favorites, and improves overall app performance.

You can find the full changelog [here](https://featurevoting.tobibechtold.dev/app/intake/changelog).
