## Gemini BYOK fixes

Intake 2.4.2 is a small maintenance update focused on Gemini chats and Health Connect.

Gemini chats that use your own API key should behave more reliably now. This update fixes issues in the Gemini BYOK flow so meal logging, corrections, and follow-up messages are less likely to get stuck for provider-specific reasons.

## Health Connect fixes

Health Connect also gets a few reliability fixes.

Syncing health data should be steadier in edge cases where Android permissions or Health Connect responses did not behave as expected. The goal is simple: Intake should keep your health data connection predictable while you track food, calories, and macros.

## Small polish

No big new features this time, just fixes after the recent Intake AI release.
