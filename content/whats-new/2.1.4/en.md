## Count only workouts and apply activity calories by percentage

You can now decide whether all activity calories from Apple Health should count toward your day, or only the workouts you started from your Apple Watch or the Fitness app.

You can also choose whether those calories should count in full or only by a custom percentage.

![Activity settings](./assets/activity-settings.mp4)

## Remember amount

Foods you have logged before now remember the amount you selected last time.

## Meal and water reminders

You can now turn on reminders for upcoming meals or for drinking water again.

For meals, you can decide individually for each meal whether you want a notification and when it should appear.

For water, you can either use fixed intervals or try the Smart mode, which looks at how much water you have already logged and decides whether you need a reminder or not. Smart mode is currently in beta.

![Notifications](./assets/notifications.mp4)

## Choose which foods to copy or import

When copying or importing meals, you can now choose whether to copy or import all foods or only selected ones.

This gives you more control over exactly which products you want to move.

![Copying](./assets/copy-selection.mp4)

## Share individual foods

You asked for it: you can now share individual foods as well.

Just add the food to a meal and use the share button in the edit sheet. The format is the same as when sharing a full meal.

![Sharing](./assets/share-products.mp4)

## Recipes in the macro overview

Recipes in the macro overview are no longer shown only as whole recipes. They are now broken down into their individual ingredients, so you can see more clearly which foods contributed to each macronutrient.

## Custom water amounts

When tracking water, you can now also log your own custom amounts easily, similar to how it works on the Apple Watch.

![Water](./assets/water-tracking.mp4)

## Change weight more easily

You no longer need to open a menu to change your weight. You can now do it directly from the dashboard.

![Weight](./assets/weight-tracking.mp4)

## Other improvements

- When customizing the Today page, you can now place cards above the dashboard
- App startup time has been significantly reduced
- There is now a confirmation prompt before deleting your own products and recipes
